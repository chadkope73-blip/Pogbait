PogBait

A simple amenity planning tool for the road. Find likely distances to coffee, fuel, groceries, outdoor supply, and more from Colorado priority areas, ZIPs, GPS, or coordinates.

PogBait helps with quick supply planning. Open the tool, enter a ZIP code, use coordinates, or pull your GPS location, then get likely distance estimates to basic amenities before you roll in.
Offline planning estimate only. Distances use local sample points for Colorado priority areas and should be treated as trip-planning guidance.
